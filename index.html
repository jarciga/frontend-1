WELCOME TO ALLUP SILICA

Allup Silica Limited (APS) is a publicly listed silica exploration Company, focused on identifying areas of the ‘right type’ of sand for the future processing of a refined purity product for the photovoltaic and other related modern industries.

Our silica sand tenements are located across a number of exploration project sites, giving us the best opportunities to develop in line with our core philosophy of ‘Projects. Purity. Ports’.

Currently, these project sites are located in both Western Australia and the Northern Territory.

Early indications are encouraging, and it is our intention to work towards achieving a commercial product, within industry specifications, for the sectors upon which we are focused. Silica is an essential commodity for many new generation industrial applications and plays a key functional role in the componentry needed to bring about sustainable energy outcomes.

We invite you to learn more about our potential to supply industry with this important resource.

Projects. Purity. Ports.

ALLUP’s commercial strategy is based on the concurrent investigation of Multiple Projects with the type,
and quality of sand that can be processed to the Purity required for use in the manufacture of photovoltaic
glass. Each individual Project will come with its own set of logistics and Port solutions.

This provides risk mitigation, scale opportunity and variability.
